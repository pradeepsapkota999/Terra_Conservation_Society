About Us

Terra Conservation Society is a non-profit making, non-governmental organisation established in 2024 in Pokhara, Nepal and registered with the District Administrative Office of Kaski (Regd. No. 4836) and affiliated with the Social Welfare Council (Regd. No. 56827).

The Terra Conservation Society is dedicated to preserving biodiversity, stabilizing our climate, and protecting wildlife habitats through collaboration with organizations and stakeholders. By uniting diverse expertise and resources, we strive to enact sustainable practices and address global environmental challenges for a harmonious coexistence between humanity and the natural world.

Our Services

Awareness Campaigns

Our Mission

  • Preserve biodiversity, stabilize climate, and protect wildlife habitats.
  • Collaborate with diverse organizations and stakeholders for effective conservation efforts.
  • Promote sustainable practices and innovations to address environmental challenges.
